# Project References

## 1. Core Model (ALBA)
*   **Title:** ALBA: A comprehensive growth model to optimize algae-bacteria wastewater treatment in raceway ponds.
*   **Authors:** Francesca Casagli, Gaetano Zuccaro, Olivier Bernard, Jean-Philippe Steyer, Elena Ficara.
*   **Journal:** *Water Research* (2021), Vol. 190, 116734.
*   **DOI/Link:** [https://doi.org/10.1016/j.watres.2020.116734](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.watres.2020.116734)
*   **Role in Project:** Provides the fundamental 19 biological processes, stoichiometric matrix, and pH sub-model.

## 2. Advanced Frameworks (M-ALBA)
*   **Title:** M-ALBA: A modelling framework to guide the optimization of membrane-assisted algae-bacteria systems.
*   **Authors:** François Crouchett-Catalán, Jineth Arango, Olivier Bernard, Carlos Martínez, Francesca Casagli, David Jeison.
*   **Journal:** *Science of The Total Environment* (2025), Vol. 969, 182655.
*   **DOI/Link:** [https://doi.org/10.1016/j.scitotenv.2025.182655](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.scitotenv.2025.182655)
*   **Role in Project:** Extends ALBA for membrane-assisted systems, useful for the "M-ALBA" module expansion.

## 3. Biomass Retention Studies
*   **Title:** Assessing the impact of biomass retention in membrane-assisted microalgae-bacteria sewage treatment.
*   **Authors:** Jineth Arango, Aníbal Rojo, Francesca Casagli, Olivier Bernard, David Jeison.
*   **Journal:** *Journal of Environmental Chemical Engineering* (2025), Vol. 13, No. 2, 115427.
*   **DOI/Link:** [https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jece.2025.115427](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jece.2025.115427)
*   **Role in Project:** Provides insights into biomass retention impact, critical for validating long-term stability in the Digital Twin.
