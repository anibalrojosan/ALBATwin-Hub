# Table of Bioprocess Rates

For the ALBA model, Liebig's Law of the Minimum is used to calculate the growth rate of each process. The general expression that describes the growth rates of the biological processes is:

$$
\rho_{i,\text{growth}} = \mu_{\text{max},i} \cdot f_{T,i} \cdot f_{pH,i} \cdot f_I \cdot \frac{K_n}{K_n + S_n} \cdot \min\left(\sum_j \frac{S_j}{K_j + S_j}\right) \cdot X_{BM,i}
\tag{4}
$$
